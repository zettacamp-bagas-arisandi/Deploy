What is Sanplayer ?


Sanplayer is music player with some unique features. You can play music without ads for 3 month for free or forever.

Available weekly charts, what song is trending right now or going viral, and which artists have released their current songs or album.

Also you can make some playlist and describe it to mood's, so whenever you are in a certain mood, just click the mood playlist that you set before.

Or search albums or just follow your favorite Artist for any updates.

and do karaoke to your favorite songs with lyric feature then publish and get rate frome other user.


The Different Between Personal vs. Free ?


Here is the information about What is the different version between Personal and Free :

Personal Version

  • No Ads Forever, choosing personal version is great thing. Because you can listening to your favorite music anytime without annoying ads.
  • Offline Playing, you can download the songs or album even playlist, so you can still play the music without internet data.
  • No limit Playlist, make playlist as you want without limitation.

Free Version

  • No Ads for 3month only, you can listening to your favorite music anytime without annoying ads, but its only 3 month.
  • Online Playing, you should use internet data for playing the music.
  • 3 Playlist only, make playlist with 3 limitation.
